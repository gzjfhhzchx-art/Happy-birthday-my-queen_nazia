To Nazia, on this day

Nazia, there are not enough words in any language to describe what you mean to me. But on your birthday, I want to try — because you deserve to hear it, over and over again, in every way I know how.

I have watched you move through this world with a grace that leaves me breathless. The way you laugh — really laugh — with your whole body. The way you listen, fully and generously, as though whoever is speaking is the most important person in the room. The way you hold beauty in small, ordinary things that others walk past without noticing.

You are, without question, the most extraordinary person I have ever known. Not because life has been easy for you — but because of how you carry yourself when it isn't. Steady. Warm. Impossibly kind.

Since you have been in my life, I have learned what it means to feel truly known. You have seen me at my most uncertain, my most tired, my most unguarded — and you have always stayed. That kind of love is rare, Nazia. You are rare.

On this birthday, I want to give you one simple truth: you are deeply, completely, endlessly loved. Not for what you do. Not for how you look. But for who you are — every quiet corner, every bright edge of you.

Here's to you, to this year, and to every year that follows. I — Farhan — will be here, beside you, for all of them.

Forever and always, yours ♡
— Farhan Labib
